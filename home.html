About Us

We have developed a web application with basic necessities which a farmer can use.The app consists of farming tips such as advising proper crops to be planted according to their field types , proper irrigation and cultivation methods , suitable fertiliser to be used for farming. A farmer can act as both user and admin in the app. In the user mode , a farmer can buy fertilisers from local stores , can learn new farming techniques and strategies, and could know all the information regarding the crops which are planted and current market prices as well.As there is very bad agricultural marketing going on , In admin mode , a farmer can directly update his availability of product in app along with prices , which can be bought directly by the users ( Transportation has to be managed by the farmer in bulk ) or by the intermediate market retailers and sell them in the market. This would help in gaining maximum profit for the farmers as the crop is sold by himself and the product could be sold with least price in the market as there are no more than 2 intermediate retailers which would reduce intermediate retailers who gain maximum profit before the product could reach the market. The app would also consists of an algorithm which allows the farmer to calculate optimal price for the product according to the investment in crops and fertilisers , time taken for cultivation and labour for optimal profits. The app comes along with the pictures which help the farmers who arenâ€™t well educated to understand the use of the application. It would support most of the regional languages so that in can be used around the country. The learning can be done through reading (with picturization ) and also through videos which would increase the understanding. The main intention of this app is to keep updating the farmer with various techniques & technology and not to depend farmers on local traders and lose profit.
